Type's of Sodium












Three compounds that have sodium in it.











    Sodium Hydrogen Carbonate

  • Sodium Hydrogen Carbonate (NaHCO3): Used in medical field to treat hyperacidity.

  • And It is called "baking soda". It is a baking powder so, it can use in making food, like baking a bread.











    • Sodium Chloride

  • Sodium Chloride (NaCl): Sodium chloride is the chemical name for salt.

  • It used to flavor and preserve foods and wash wounds











    • Sodium Carbonate

  • Sodium Carbonate (Na2CO3): Used in making light industry, daily chemicals, building materials, chemical industry, food industry, etc.

  • It has a name called "washing soda" and is used in cleaning products, glass production.
