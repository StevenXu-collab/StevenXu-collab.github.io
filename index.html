About the Sodium

  • Sodium is widely distributed in nature, and with a lot of reserves. Sodium usually exists in a compound form, because it is so reactive. It is found in compounds such as sodium chloride or salt. Sodium chloride is found in ocean water (salt water) and salt lakes.

  • Sodium is the sixth most common element on Earth, and makes up 2.6% of the Earth's crust.

  • Sodium is a chemical element with the symbol Na and It's atomic number is 11.

  • Sodium is an essential element for all animals and some plants.
